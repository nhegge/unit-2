U.S. CO2 Emissions from Energy Production

Total energy emissions from each State from: 1960 to 2023

The United States, to this day, continues to lead the world in total CO2 emissions. However, these emissions are not evenly or proportionally distributed across each State. Because of this, this map was created to allow users to sequence through and examine total CO2 emissions from yearly energy consumption from each State (measured in million metric tons of carbon dioxide CO2).

Use the sequence controls (the slider/arrows) in the lower-left corner to observe CO2 how emissions look each year and how they have changed. Click on the red circles to retrieve detailed data on emissions for State and year. Use the filter slider to filter States by minimum yearly CO2 emissions.
